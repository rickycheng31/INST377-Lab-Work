How to make a sandwich

You will need the following to make a sandwich

  • bread
  • turkey
  • cheese

These are additional toppings you can add to your sammy:

  1. lettuce
  2. tomatoes
  3. ketchup
  4. onions
  5. mustard

How to combine the different ingredients into a sandwich:

First you toast the bread,
Then you melt the cheese,
Last you layer the meat on top.
